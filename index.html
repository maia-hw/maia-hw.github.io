HOW TO PLAY

Use the colours to guess the hidden word in the Wordle solution! Assume the Wordle player is playing in hard mode - they always use all revealed hints.

The solution to the Wordle is "inane". The player guessed "lapse" and one other word before solving the Wordle. Our goal is to figure out what the hidden guess is.

We know:

  • The green squares in the hidden guess match the solution: the hidden guess ends in "n" and "e".
  • The white squares in the hidden guess do not contain letters that are in the solution.
  • The yellow square in the hidden guess must be a letter that is in the solution, but in a different spot.
  • The user does not repeat incorrect letters: the hidden guess will not contain "l", "p", or "s".
  • The user includes all known letters in their future guesses: the hidden guess must contain an "a", but in a different spot.
This means the hidden guess must be "atone"!
